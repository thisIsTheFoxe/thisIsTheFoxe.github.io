How to install

Once you clicked the install-link or scanned the QR code, you should see an alert that asks you to install the app. Once you tap "Install" the app is being installed on the background. You should find it on your home screen.
Before you can open it you will have to trust the HUGO BOSS developer certificate, otherwise you will get an error like: "Untrusted enterprise Developer". If you get an error like this, go to your Settings app. In the section General -> Profiles & Device Management you will see the HUGO BOSS certificate. Tap on the certificate and then on "Trust HUGO BOSS AG". You will be asked one more time to confirm this action and then you are ready. Now you should be able to open the QualityFeedback App on your home screen. For further help see also Apple Support
